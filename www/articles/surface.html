Microsoft Surface



Surprisingly, Microsoft products (past the Surface 3) make for decent Linux computers. It does take some tinkering and tweaking, but there are a plethora of resources online for this sort of thing.

Main Issues:

  • Cameras
  • Detatchable Keyboard
  • Battery
  • Docks
  • Dedicated GPUs

The distro of choice for most Surface hackers is Ubuntu, and due to the sheer number of resources available for that distro, it's probably best for beginners.That being said, many people have been able to make Manjaro, Linux Mint, and other Arch and Ubuntu-based distros work well.
