Dell XPS 9560



The XPS 9560 is a solid machine used by many students, professionals, and enthusiasts alike. It is a solid Linux machine, provided its proprietary features can be wrangled.

Main Issues:

  • RAID Storage configuration
  • Power Management
  • nVidia GPUs
  • Secure boot
  • 4K display
  • Wireless

Many people have managed to get a wide variety of distros working on this machine, with varying levels of success. Experienced Linux users can deside for themselves which of these to use in the long run.




For beginners, however, it seems that, due to its native support for GPUs, Pop!_OS is a good choice for people who want a "just works" Linux experience.

Pop!_OS is a distro focused on ease of use. With a brilliant spin on the GNOME desktop environment and an impressive amount of effort put into things like power management and graphics switching technology, Pop!_OS works well on machines with uncooperative graphics systems and other bizarre technologies.
